Communication Terms
1. Purpose of Communication

By contacting TruthGuard, you agree that your communication may be used to improve our services, provide you with support, and help combat misinformation globally.

2. Response Time Commitment

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 4 hours during business days (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST). Urgent requests receive priority attention.

3. Privacy & Data Protection

Your contact information will only be used to respond to your inquiry and will never be shared with third parties. All communications are encrypted and secured.

4. Content Guidelines

Please ensure your messages are respectful and relevant to TruthGuard's mission of combating misinformation. We reserve the right to refuse service for inappropriate content.

5. Data Usage

Anonymous, aggregated data from communications may be used to improve our AI models and detection algorithms, always respecting your privacy.
